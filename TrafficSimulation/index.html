Traffic simulation based on Carla

Playspeed × 2


Driver models with different driving styles. The one on the right is more aggressive than the one on the left, e.g., has a higher probability of changing lanes.

Highway exit ramp: the leading vehicle rushes to exit the ramp from the far left firstly (left); the red vehicle in the middle merges into the right lane, and the black vehicle behind gives way (right).

Mixed traffic scenarios: Pedestrians give way to motorcycles passing at high speed (initial); Pedestrians cross the road, and the purple vehicle give way (end).

Traffic flow at intersection.
