Failure scenarios on the both benchmarks. (Playspeed × 2)


Although the shortcut eliminator prevents the planner from taking shortcuts based on low-dimensional features, the excessively rapid startup cannot effectively manage emergencies and slightly compromises comfort.

The purple trajectory represents the target set of points that the planner aims to reach after executing multiple lane changes. However, the planner's input does not account for the mission goal and lacks an effective lane change strategy. It becomes disoriented at the intersection, resulting in a collision.

The planner has not yet reached human-level driving capabilities and struggles to handle extreme scenarios, such as driving against traffic to avoid unexpected situations.
